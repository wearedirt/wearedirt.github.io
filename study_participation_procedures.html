STEP 1: Preparing the DIRT watch before participating in a research study

  1. Wear the DIRT watch on your left wrist and tighten the strap to your comfort level. Please wear the watch comfortably - the strap should not feel too tight!

  2. Plug in the white data collection cable. Please note the "B" on the micro-usb plug should face up as shown below.

  3. Take out the sticky pads from its packaging and place two stickies onto your fingers on your left hand as shown below (one pad on your index finger, one pad on your middle finger)

  4. Snap each of the cable onto the sticky pads on your fingers using the metal nipples.

  5. You are all good to go! Please make sure the white cable is not too extended (feel free to adjust the watch up and down your wrist for comfort). You can place your hand down on your lap in a comfortable position. Please do not put pressure onto the white cable and your fingers as this would introduce noise into the data. Continue below to start a new study in the DIRT App.

  1. Open the DIRT Research App

  2. Enter the provided Study Code into the "Study Code" box. Then, press "Select Device" to continue.

STEP 3: Finishing a study

At the end of all studies, you will need to upload your data. You will see the following instructions in the app.

To start the data upload process, first remove the DIRT watch from your wrist.
  1. Unplug the white data collection cable from your DIRT watch.
  2. Peel off the sticky pads and press them together like the image below
  3. Hold on to the sticky pads and remove the cable from the pads by unsnapping them off. You can throw away the sticky pads, but store the data collection cable somewhere safe for future study participation.
  4. Take off your DIRT watch
Once you take off your watch, plug it in.
When your watch is plugged in, you can now start the data upload
You will see this screen while your data is uploading.
