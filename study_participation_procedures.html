SECTION 5: Study Participation Procedures

To successfully participate in a DIRT research study, please fully read through all the sections below. Please also make sure your DIRT watch is charged before starting your participation.

⚠️ Warning: When you are actively participating in a study with the DIRT App, please close out of all other applications and internet browsers. These extra processes running in the background may cause the DIRT App to crash during a study due to hardware limitations.
